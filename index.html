Why we keep the kitchen

The hard builds are ours. Never the customer's.

A build we own
An asset.
A build they own
A liability with our name on the support contract.
Hand-built by a thousand customers, it breaks at the next upgrade, and our phone rings at 2am. That's how a product company quietly becomes a consulting company — and the market never pays those the same.
The economics — one decision, seen twice

Hand-build every Copilot and costs rise with every sale — a treadmill. Assemble and generate, and the cost of a normal deal falls toward zero and stays there as you grow.

This quarter's custom becomes next quarter's menu item. The system gets cheaper the longer it runs.
So this is the call

Customers order. We cook.
